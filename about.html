Our Story

Christie Café was born from a love of coffee, community, and comfort. Nestled in the heart of Victoria Island, Lagos, our café is more than just a place to grab a drink — it's a space to connect, unwind, and feel at home.

From our handcrafted brews to our locally sourced pastries, every detail is curated with care. Whether you're catching up with friends, working remotely, or simply enjoying a quiet moment, Christie Café welcomes you with warmth and flavor.

We believe in brewing joy — one cup, one conversation, one experience at a time.

Our Journey
